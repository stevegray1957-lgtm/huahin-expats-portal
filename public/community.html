📅 Regular Expat Events

Hua Hin has something happening every week. Here are the regular events that expats love most.

Mon
WK

Hash House Harriers — Weekly Run/Walk

Every Monday. Different location each week. 1–2 hour run or walk through Hua Hin's countryside, followed by social drinks. All fitness levels welcome.

Sport & Social
Thu
WK

Quiz Nights — Various Venues

Multiple bars run quiz nights on Thursdays. Entry usually free or small fee. Teams of 2–6 people. Great for meeting new people. Check Expats in Hua Hin Facebook for this week's venue.

Social
Sat
WK

Weekend Market — Soi 72

Every Saturday and Sunday morning. Huge local market with fresh produce, street food and household goods. Great for exploring local culture and meeting Thai neighbours.

Market
Sat
WK

Cicada Night Market — Khao Takiab

Friday and Saturday evenings near Khao Takiab. Art, crafts, live music, street food. One of Thailand's most atmospheric night markets. Very popular with expats.

Culture
Jan
ANN

Hua Hin Music Festival

Annual free music festival at Khao Takiab beach. Usually January or February. Multiple stages, Thai and international acts. The social event of the expat year.

Culture
Jun
ANN

Hua Hin Jazz Festival

Annual jazz festival on the beach in June. Free entry. World-class jazz acts and a fantastic atmosphere. One of Thailand's best music events.
