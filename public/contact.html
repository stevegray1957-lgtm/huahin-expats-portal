About HuaHin ExpatsPortal

HuaHin ExpatsPortal is the go-to online resource for English-speaking expats living in or moving to Hua Hin, Thailand. We provide free, unbiased information on visas, cost of living, healthcare, banking, accommodation and daily life.

Our directory lists 260+ verified local businesses across 19 categories — from hospitals and law firms to restaurants and real estate agents. Every listing has been researched and can be reviewed and updated by the community.

We are an independent website, not affiliated with any government body, tourism board or commercial organisation. We earn a small commission from some affiliate links (clearly labelled) which helps fund the site. All editorial content is produced independently.
